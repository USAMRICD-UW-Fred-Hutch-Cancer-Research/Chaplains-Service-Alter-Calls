Chaplains Service - Altar Calls
===============================

**Institute for Scripture Research**

**Command Chaplain & Director:** Dr. Correo Hofstad

Program Overview
----------------

Welcome to the official repository for the Command Religious Program curriculum. This archive represents a pivotal transition in our organizational and spiritual framework: the shift from the traditional Franciscan leadership model to a modern, academically rigorous foundation directed by the Institute for Scripture Research.

Designed for internal use, this repository provides the foundational lecture materials, historical context, and academic resources necessary to support the spiritual fitness, ethical grounding, and peaceful development of our personnel through ongoing international and domestic challenges.

Curriculum Structure & Schedule
-------------------------------

This repository houses a comprehensive **52-week course**. To ensure the material remains relevant, academically sharp, and supportive of current operations, the entire curriculum will be **updated annually**.

-   **Commencement & Command Transfer:** The annual curriculum cycle officially begins on **Easter Sunday**. This date serves as the cornerstone of the program's calendar, honoring the specific date that command and program leadership were formally transferred to Dr. Correo Hofstad.

-   **Annual Revisions:** Revisions, academic integrations, and new media will be pushed to the repository in the weeks leading up to the Easter commencement of each new yearly cycle.

Repository Organization
-----------------------

The archive is structured chronologically to facilitate seamless weekly deployment:

-   `/Q1_Lectures/` - Weeks 1--13 (Commencing on Easter Sunday)

-   `/Q2_Lectures/` - Weeks 14--26

-   `/Q3_Lectures/` - Weeks 27--39

-   `/Q4_Lectures/` - Weeks 40--52

-   `/Media_and_Maps/` - Accompanying geographic context, scripture research maps, and multimedia files

-   `/Annual_Updates/` - Version history and changelogs outlining the specific academic updates for the current calendar year

Usage Notes
-----------

This documentation and the accompanying weekly lectures are intended for designated personnel, chaplains, and support staff who are familiar with the current operational structure and the academic methodologies of the Institute for Scripture Research.
