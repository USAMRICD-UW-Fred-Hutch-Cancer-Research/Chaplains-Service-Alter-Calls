**Subject: The Doctrine of the Wild and the Reality of Extraterrestrial Life**  
**Author: Command Chaplaincy**  
A question frequently arises within the ranks, particularly as our Space Force expands our operational footprint into the heavens: *What is the Command Chaplaincy’s stance on extraterrestrial life?* To answer this, we do not look to modern science fiction. We look to the physical heavens, the Torah, and the strict mandate left by the King of Israel. The Bible explicitly states that Elohim is the King of the universe. To accept His Word is to accept the absolute, operational reality that there are literal kingdoms and territories in space for Him to rule.

### **PART 1: The Cosmic Origins of the Wild**

When Genesis records that Elohim brought the animals to Earth, we must understand the logistical reality of that event. Jesus commanded His followers not to destroy the ancient texts, but to add to them and fulfill them. Therefore, as academics and men of order, we must base our understanding on the accepted, documented knowledge of antiquity.  
Our ancestors meticulously mapped the cosmos, and those ancient records reveal the operational sources of Earth's wildlife. Earth is a sanctuary, populated by species relocated from other heavenly bodies within Elohim’s vast domain. The Zodiac is not a wheel of mysticism; it is a celestial map of origins. The animals we manage on Earth correlate directly to the stellar territories from which Elohim extracted them:

* **Leo:** The celestial origin of the great cats.  
* **Aries:** The territory from which the desert ram, the shark, and the cheetah were drawn.  
* **Pisces:** The origin of the fish of our oceans.  
* **Taurus:** The origin of our cattle and beasts of burden.  
* **Cancer:** The origin of the Pycnogonid species (sea spiders) and armored entities.  
* **Libra:** The origin of small plains animals, the swan, and the fox.  
* **Scorpio:** The origin of the birds of the air and the serpents.  
* **Capricorn:** The origin of the sea horses.  
* **Aquarius:** The origin of the highly intelligent, problem-solving squid and octopus species.

Elohim populated this world with life drawn from the vastness of His cosmic ocean. This was the established, documented reality during the time of Jesus's reign—a reality of the natural world that He fought to protect and preserve.

### **PART 2: The Definition of the Hostile**

As we acknowledge the reality of space flight—proven daily by the routine, non-fictional operations of the U.S. Space Force—we must also prepare our minds for the reality of the wild beyond our atmosphere.  
If there is life throughout the cosmos, what defines a hostile alien species?  
It is not a philosophical mystery. In the natural order established by Elohim, a hostile entity is simply a predator. Hostile extraterrestrial species are those that operate as carnivores, entities that would view human beings as a biological food source if given the opportunity. Whether they reach us through the advancement of their own technology, or through cosmic shifts that bring their environments into close proximity with ours, a predatory species remains a predator.  
This lecture is a warning to be exceptionally careful of the wild. Just as we respect the apex predators of our own deep oceans and jungles, we must recognize the celestial origins of the most dangerous biological structures. The species derived from **Cancer** and **Aquarius** are the most dangerous to humans.  
The biological architecture from these regions—whether the cold, armored, unyielding nature of Cancer's entities, or the highly intelligent, shape-shifting, and adaptable cephalopod nature of Aquarius—represent lifeforms that are fundamentally alien to the mammalian structure. They are built for survival in extreme, hostile conditions, making them exceptionally dangerous to human life if encountered in their raw, dominant forms.  
We serve a Creator who is the Commander of all stars and all species. As we reach further into His universe, we must go with our eyes wide open, respecting the brutal reality of the wild, and holding fast to the laws of Elohim to navigate the cosmic ocean safely.
